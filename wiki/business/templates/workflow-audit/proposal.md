# A one-week audit of [Client]'s repeatable workflows

Prepared for [PRIMARY CONTACT NAME], [TITLE]
Prepared by Overlook Strategy
Valid through [DATE + 30 DAYS]

---

## What we are proposing

A one-week engagement in which the studio walks [Client]'s repeatable white-collar workflows, identifies the three to five that would absorb the most senior-staff time if automated, prototypes the rubric the studio would use to score those automations, and delivers a written audit document with three forward-path recommendations.

If the audit recommends moving forward, the studio's Skill Pack Build (three weeks) and Always-On Retainer (monthly) are described in section [Investment] below. The audit stands on its own. There is no obligation to take the next step.

## Why now

The frontier model release in late 2025 quietly shifted the line on what an agent can do without a human watching it. A specific set of repeatable office tasks — drafting summaries, enriching leads, preparing recurring reports, reviewing structured documents — now run autonomously to the same standard a senior associate would deliver, at roughly five percent of the labor cost.

Most companies have not yet sorted which of their workflows are in that set and which are not. The audit exists to answer that question for [Client] in writing, with names attached.

## What you receive at the end of the week

A single document, roughly twenty-five pages, that contains:

- An inventory of every recurring workflow we observed
- A two-axis map sorting those workflows by readiness for automation today
- A ranked shortlist of three to five candidate skills
- One example rubric, fully specified, showing how the studio would keep an automated workflow honest at scale
- Three forward-path options with concrete numbers
- Two appendices: workflow detail and interview notes

You also receive a sixty-minute readout call on the Friday of the engagement to walk the document and answer questions.

## What we will need from you

- One ninety-minute intake call with the primary contact at the start of the week
- Three sixty-minute interviews with staff you nominate, covering the domains we will scope on the intake call
- Read access to up to ten documents you nominate
- One sixty-minute readout call on the Friday of the engagement

Total time commitment from [Client]'s side: roughly five hours across the week, distributed across [PRIMARY CONTACT] and three nominated staff.

## What it costs

Three options at the audit tier. Pick the one that matches the depth of the question [Client] is trying to answer.

| Tier | What's included | Investment |
|---|---|---|
| Audit | Five business days, one document, three forward-path recommendations, readout call | $1,500 |
| Audit+ | Audit scope plus a follow-up working session and one revision pass on the deliverable | $3,000 |
| Audit + retainer | Audit+ scope plus three months of advisory ($750 / month) for ongoing follow-up | $3,000 + $750 / month |

The audit is fixed-fee. There are no hourly overages.

## What follows the audit (only if the audit recommends it)

The audit exits to one of three forward paths. Two of the three are studio engagements; the first is "do nothing."

| Forward path | Investment | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Path A — do nothing | $0 | n/a |
| Path B — Skill Pack Build | $7,500 – $15,000 | three weeks from kickoff |
| Path C — Skill Pack Build + Always-On Retainer | $7,500 – $15,000 + $1,500 – $4,000 / month | three weeks from kickoff, then monthly |

The audit deliverable shows the math under each path against [Client]'s observed workflow set. The studio recommends one path in writing. [Client] picks.

## How the studio works

We work with a small number of clients a year. Every engagement starts on the bluff in Ventura — phone off, notebook open — and ends with a system the team can actually run. We are not a generalist AI consultancy. We are a brand-and-systems studio that happens to be early on the agentic-engineering side of that work.

Three things [Client] should know about how we operate.

We never auto-publish. Every output produced inside an engagement passes through a human review step before it reaches an external recipient. This is structural, not configurable.

We do not mark up infrastructure. Model API charges, hosting, and any pass-through tooling are billed at cost.

We are honest about what doesn't work yet. The audit names workflows we would not automate today. If the answer is "wait six months," that's the answer.
