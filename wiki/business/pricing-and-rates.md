## Tiered project pricing (April 2026)

First time tiered pricing has been published anywhere. Lives on the three [[overlook-vertical-landing-pages|vertical landing pages]] generated 2026-04-26. Hosting / Sanity / ecommerce platform fees pass through at cost on every tier.

| Vertical | Foundation tier | Mid tier | Premium tier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Yacht broker** | Foundation $6,500 (8wk) | Brokerage $14,000 (10wk) | Fleet $26,000 (14wk) |
| **Boutique vacation rental** | Cabin $5,500 (6wk) | Property $11,500 (9wk) | Portfolio $22,000 (14wk) |
| **Winery / tasting room** | Tasting Room $7,500 (7wk) | Club $15,000 (10wk) | Estate $28,000 (14wk) |

**Winery founder pricing:** first two California winery clients get 30% off any tier in exchange for case study rights. Good through Q3 2026.

> [!warning] Pricing not settled — second pass (April 27) proposes a ~30% lower tier
> A second run of the [[overlook-vertical-landing-pages-2026-04-27|landing-page generator]] on April 27, 2026 produced an alternate pricing structure: Foundation $3,800–$4,800 / Standard $7,500–$9,500 / Showcase $14,500–$18,000+, plus sportfishing charter as the third vertical instead of winery (with 20% founding-client pricing on the first three sportfishing projects). Both runs are unshipped. The pricing on this page reflects the April 26 numbers; if Finn picks the April 27 batch as canonical, this section needs a rewrite. Decision tracked on [[overlook-vertical-landing-pages]].

**Implications:**
- Existing clients ([[rustler-42]], [[somliøya|Sømliøya]], [[all-in-one-music]]) were quoted before this pricing existed. Worth back-checking they're inside these bands; if they're below, no action — they were early. If significantly above, no action — premium delivered.
- The Foundation tier on each vertical sits at the entry point a small business can rationalize. Anything below $5,500 likely needs to become a per-task line item ([[per-task-pricing]]).
- Brokerage / Property / Club mid-tier is the "expected" engagement — most leads should land here.

## Pricing models worth considering

The 2026-04-25 [[isenberg-future-of-saas-30-step|Isenberg ingest]] adds a model that doesn't currently exist on Overlook's invoices: **per-task pricing** for repeatable agent-executed workflows. See [[per-task-pricing]] for the full breakdown. Worth piloting on a single Overlook workflow (e.g., monthly client portal update production) as a $X/post line item on top of existing retainers, to see if the per-task economics work for a human Finn before any agentization.

This wouldn't replace project pricing or retainers — it would add a third tier specifically for repeatable workflows. Tracked as an idea seed in [[Finn-OS/ideas/backlog]].
