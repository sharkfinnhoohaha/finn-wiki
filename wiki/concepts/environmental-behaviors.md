## TL;DR

The design philosophy guiding [[three-altitudes]]. **Pivot away from "Artifacts"** (3D models of planes, pre-amps) **toward atmospheric reactivity** — the site itself reacts to the user to communicate identity. Stages convey identity, not project cards.

## The pivot quote

> "We are pivoting away from 'Artifacts' and moving toward Environmental Behaviors. Instead of 3D models of planes or pre-amps, the site will communicate who you are through how it reacts to the user."

## The cinematic frame

> "The site should feel like a short film directed by the user's scroll wheel."

## Stage-based identity communication

Four sticky scroll zones (4 × 200vh = 800vh total), each mapped to a facet of the [[identity-and-brand-architecture|Finn Bennett identity]]:

| Zone | Scroll % | Identity facet | Atmosphere component |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shoreline | 0–25 | Surfer / Ventura origin | `ShorelineAtmosphere` (48×24 wave plane, sin displacement 0.18–0.38 Hz, 28 foam particles) |
| Pocket | 26–50 | Producer / drummer | `PocketAtmosphere` (65 amber CylinderGeometry filaments, snare-beat pulse `pow(sin(t * π * 4.0), 8)`) |
| Engine Room | 51–75 | Developer | `EngineRoomAtmosphere` (14 grey wireframe modules, glassmorphism) + ghosting code |
| Horizon | 76–100 | Pilot | `HorizonAtmosphere` (3 line objects forming flight horizon, mouseRef lerp 0.04 inertia) |

## Supporting atmospheric pieces

- **`GhostingCode`** component — 7 TypeScript/Next.js fragments, RAF loop with direct DOM `style.transform` writes (no React re-renders) for performance
- **`TransitionPass`** — GLSL wave-wipe shader bell at 0.25 (was 0.33), ambient water refraction in Shoreline, clean pass starts at 0.72
- **`MediaLayers`** — `wave-teal.png` overlay (Stage 1, opacity 0.14, mix-blend-mode overlay, fade 22–32%), `wave-transition.mp4` (plays once at 24–32% scroll, resets at 18%), `code-bg.mp4` (Stage 3, opacity 0.06, mix-blend-mode screen)

## Why "environmental" beats "artifact"

The artifact approach (3D plane model, 3D pre-amp model) is literal — it shows what Finn does. Environmental behaviors are **expressive** — they convey *how it feels* to be Finn doing each thing:

- "Physics of the Swell: Stage 1 must feel 'heavy' and fluid like a Pacific swell."

The aesthetic vocabulary in [[identity-and-brand-architecture]] (Pacific swell, snare beat, filament light, ghosting code, horizon tilt, 16mm film grain) was generated by this philosophy.

## Direct quotes

> "Identity First: The name 'Finn Bennett' is the anchor. 'Overlook Strategy' and 'Overlook Audio' are sub-modules — outputs of his creative logic, not the primary identity."

> "I have added some assets (photos and videos) to the website folder. Can you put them where they need to be/incorporate them as you deem fit?"

(Finn delegates aesthetic placement once the philosophy is set — trust scales when the principle is clear.)
