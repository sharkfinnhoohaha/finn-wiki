## File-org learnings

From `local_9ec480d0-d4ce-43c2-92ba-bc90476f280d`:

- macOS screen recordings/screenshots use U+202F (narrow no-break space), not regular space — globbing for spaces misses them
- Skip hashing files >500MB; name+size is enough for big media
- 6 high-confidence dupes quarantined; versioned mix files left alone
