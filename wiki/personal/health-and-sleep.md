## TL;DR

Sleep tracked through the Life OS database. **Average ~6.5 hrs**, **burnout flag at 5 hr nights**. Health is one of the six fixed Life OS Areas alongside Finance, Overlook Strategy, Overlook Audio, School, Aviation.

## Sleep baseline

- Average: ~6.5 hrs
- Burnout threshold: 5 hr nights flagged

## Life OS Areas (vocabulary, fixed)

The six top-level buckets used across the `Life OS - March (1)` database, [[csv-to-notion-pipeline|CSV imports]], and views:

1. Finance (see [[finance-tracking]])
2. Overlook Strategy (see [[overlook-strategy-positioning]])
3. Overlook Audio (see [[overlook-audio-positioning]])
4. School (see [[school-berklee]])
5. Aviation (see [[aviation-training]])
6. Health

(Ventura Forward also appears as a sub-Area for the [[pricing-and-rates|retainer client]].)

## Categories that touch health

From the CSV schema (`Date,Item,Area,Category,Status,Amount,Hours,Note`) — `Sleep` is a Category that lands in the Health Area, with `Hours` populated.
