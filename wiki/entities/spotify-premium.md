# Spotify Premium Student

Discounted music streaming tier for verified students.

## Finn's status

✓ Activated. Locks in the cheap rate for up to 4 cumulative annual verifications.

## What it actually is

- $6.99/mo (US) — bundles **Hulu (with ads)** in the US market
- Showtime was removed from the bundle in late 2024
- Available for up to **4 years cumulative**, not 4 calendar years from sign-up — must re-verify every 12 months. After 4 successful verifications, plan ends and converts to standard Premium pricing
- SheerID verification against `.edu` ([[berklee-online|Berklee Online]] qualifies — see [[school-berklee]])

## Re-verification mechanic

The 4-year clock counts re-verifications, not calendar time. If verification fails for any reason mid-cycle (e.g., between Berklee terms, SheerID hiccup), the plan can pause or downgrade to standard Premium until re-verified. Worth setting a reminder ~30 days before each annual re-verify so it doesn't lapse silently.

## Notes

- Apply at https://www.spotify.com/us/student/
- Spotify MCP is connected for Claude sessions per [[CLAUDE]] — playback control and playlist creation work via the connector
- $5.99 was the historical US price; current US rate is $6.99/mo (verified April 2026)
