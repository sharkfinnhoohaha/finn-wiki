# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator. Founding member of OpenAI, former Senior Director of AI at Tesla (led Autopilot), author of the nanoGPT, nanochat, and LLM101n educational projects.

## Relevance to this wiki

Originator of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]]. Published the [[karpathy-llm-wiki-gist|LLM Wiki gist]] on April 4, 2026, framing it not as a tool but as an idea file intended to be shared with an LLM agent and instantiated in collaboration.

Karpathy's own practice, as reported in the gist and [[nate-herk-claude-code-video|Nate Herk's video]]:
- Roughly 100 articles ingested
- ~500K words across the wiki
- No embedding-based retrieval, uses [[index-file-navigation]]
- Obsidian as the front end

## Other relevant work

Not directly tied to the wiki pattern but part of the same thinking:
- [[karpathy-autoresearch|Autoresearch]] — autonomous ML training-loop optimization on a single GPU. Released April 2026; widely cited as the operational counterpart to the LLM wiki's *acting* posture (vs. the wiki's *remembering* posture).
- [[andrej-karpathy|nanoGPT]], nanochat, LLM101n — educational codebases used as targets by autoresearch.
- Widely-cited takes on agent-assisted coding, the "slopacolypse," and 2026 as a year of AI productivity theater alongside real gains.
- Indirect: Sutskever's *"age of research"* reframing in [[notebooklm-overlook-strategy]] aligns with the autoresearch direction.
