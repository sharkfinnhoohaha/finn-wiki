# Greg Isenberg

Founder, advisor, podcaster — host of the Startup Ideas podcast. Self-described former adviser to TikTok and Reddit; built and sold three venture-backed companies. In 2026 he runs a content-and-community operation around startup ideas, founder education, and what he calls "the future of SaaS."

- **Howie Liu / HyperAgent conversation** ([[isenberg-howieliu-hyperagent-2026-04-29]]): the agentic-engineering studio framing that informs [[agentic-services-positioning]].
