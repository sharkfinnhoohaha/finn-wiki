## TL;DR

Tech-curation newsletter network. **>$10M in 2025 from advertising alone. 9 newsletters. 7M+ subscribers. 22 FTE all-remote.** Per-placement primary $15K–$18K. Run by Dan Ni.

The reference for **newsletter-only revenue economics with curation as the product**. ~10–30% original / 70–90% curated content quadrant per the [[human-moat-pattern|original-to-aggregated ratio]]. Not Pier and Point's model (which targets 70–80% original) but worth knowing as the upper bound for what newsletter-curation alone can sustain.
