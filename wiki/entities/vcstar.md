# VCStar (Ventura County Star)

Gannett-owned daily, the de facto Ventura paper of record. Operationally hollow: ~8–12 working journalists for an 850K-population county, supplemented by USA Today wire.

Hard metered paywall at $19.99/month or $69.99/year. Print distributed by mail; subscribers report Sunday–Tuesday issues arriving Thursday.

Gannett parent companywide cut from ~21,255 staff post-GateHouse merger to ~11,200 by Dec 2022, with continued layoffs 2023–2025 (3% August 2023, product-team March 2025, Cambridge MA November 2025, Sterling Heights printing closure 2025). Newsroom capacity degradation accelerated year-over-year.

**Primary (nominal) competitor for [[pier-and-point]]**, but the competitive moat is thin — VCStar's hollowness creates the market opening. No daily newsletter, no Ventura podcast, no data journalism, no original source-based investigation cadence.
