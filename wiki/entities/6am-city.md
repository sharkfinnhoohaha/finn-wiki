## TL;DR

Greenville, SC-based newsletter-first hyperlocal news network. Founded 2016 by Ryan Heafy + Ryne Johnston. Runs **24+ core markets** at ~3.4 headcount/city (two newsletter editors + paid intern + sales executive), centralized engineering / design / audience development. **The reference comp for Pier and Point's monetization model**, particularly the newsletter-first economics and the unit-economics math.

URL: <https://6amcity.com>

## Numbers

- **Revenue**: $3.6M (2021) → $6.3M (2022) → ~$8M (2023)
- **Capital raised**: $15.8M total. Series A Feb 2024 led by TEGNA
- **Headcount at Series A**: ~120 employees
- **Subscribers**: 1.4M newsletter subscribers across 26 core markets; **~2M after Good Daily acquisition pushed them to 400+ cities**
- **Unit economics (Heafy stated)**: $250K–$300K to launch a market; $60K marketing → 50K subscribers in 6 months; **70% margin within 36 months; $500K revenue threshold for viability**

## Stack

Fully custom CMS — Heafy describes a "six-figure annual investment." Sailthru by Marigold for sending. CitySpark for listings. Cloudflare CDN.

## The pivot pattern

**July 2025 acquired Good Daily** (350+ AI-generated newsletters from one operator covering small towns across 47 states). Matthew Henderson — Good Daily's founder — became 6AM's VP of Engineering. **Henderson's old network now operates as 6AM's "Seed Markets"**: AI-empowered newsletters in small towns mature into human-staffed core markets.

**This is the "Seed market → Core market" lifecycle** the [[automated-news-playbook-civic-operators|compass artifact]] highlights as the dominant architecture for AI-assisted hyperlocal news in 2026. Worth tracking as a possible exit path for Pier and Point if the venture works — alternatively, as a competitor model if 6AM expands into Ventura.

## Why it matters for Pier and Point

The April 25 [[pier-and-point-monetization]] page already cites 6AM as the newsletter-first comp. The April 28 compass artifact reinforces this with deeper unit-economics numbers + the Seed-to-Core lifecycle pattern. The honest comparison:

| | 6AM City core market | Pier and Point (planned) |
|---|---|---|
| Cost to launch | $250K–$300K | $80–$400/mo (~$1.5–5K/yr) |
| Headcount | ~3.4 per market | 1 (Finn) |
| Newsletter cadence | 1 daily | 1 daily |
| Time to viability | 36 months @ $500K | unclear; LION-median $148K Y1, ~$60K break-even |
| Margin at maturity | 70% | unknown — solo, no model yet |

The compass artifact's framing: **6AM is the staffed-team benchmark, not the solo target.** Pier and Point should compress 6AM's $250–300K launch cost to $30–60K / Y1 by replacing most editorial labor with AI. Realistic 3-year ceiling: $200K–$800K (per the compass artifact's solo-operator framing), not the $1M+ band 6AM core markets reach.
