## TL;DR

Independent news-credibility ratings + AI-content-farm tracking. Running count of unreliable AI-generated news sites rose from **49 in May 2023 to 3,006 AI content farms in 16 languages by 2026**. **NewsGuard + Comscore estimate $2.6B/year of programmatic ad revenue is unintentionally directed to misinformation news sites**, with 141 blue-chip brands found advertising on AI content farms in a two-month window.

The detection infrastructure that exposed (or would have exposed) every operator in [[ai-news-failures-curriculum]]. Brand-safety vendors and ad networks reference NewsGuard ratings.

URL: <https://www.newsguardtech.com>

## Why it matters for Pier and Point

NewsGuard is one of the **first surfaces sponsors will check** before signing local ad deals. A clean NewsGuard rating is a precondition for direct sponsorship at any premium CPM. Pier and Point's [[transparency-premium-as-traffic-strategy|disclosure pattern]] should be designed to score well on NewsGuard's nine criteria from launch — which means: published ethics policy, transparent ownership, named editor, corrections page, clear distinction between news and opinion, etc.
