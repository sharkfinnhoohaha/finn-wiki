# KCLU

Cal Lutheran NPR affiliate, ~90,000 listeners, 5 FT + 3 PT staff. Audio sibling to local print/digital news, but produces no text franchise of its own.

Exposed to federal CPB funding cuts. Produces no daily written reporting; content is broadcast-only. Represents untapped partnership opportunity.

Recommended **partnership target for [[pier-and-point]]**: audio swap of council recaps (KCLU airs the audio clip, [[pier-and-point]] links to the transcript), joint events, shared emergency-alert coordination.

KCLU reaches an older demographic; [[pier-and-point]] newsletter reaches younger/digital-native readers. Coverage complementary, not redundant.
