# Brad Hatfield

Composer (BMI), 100% writer credit on **"Touch of Blue"** — one of the tracks Finn used for his Berklee Music Supervision assignment. Publisher is **Brad Hatfield Music Collective** (BMI, 100%). Performed by the [[john-depaola-quartet]]; the album has both a guitar and a trumpet version of the song, which Finn flagged as needing disambiguation.
