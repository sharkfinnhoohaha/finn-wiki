# Beehiiv

Newsletter platform with **0% subscription fee** (Substack takes 10%). Built-in referral program, native ad network, Boosts for paid amplification.

Pricing: free tier → Grow ($39/month at 10K subs). No cut taken from memberships or sponsorships you sell directly, unlike Substack. Recommended in [[pier-and-point-research]] for the daily Ventura morning newsletter wedge.

## Company numbers (April 2026)

Per the [[automated-news-playbook-civic-operators|April 28 compass artifact]]:

- **$24K (2021) → $7M (2023) → $30M ARR by June 2025**
- ~110–142 staff, ~90,000 publishers
- Built by **Tyler Denk**, Morning Brew's founding engineer
- **Winning publishers from Substack** because operators own their audience rather than feeding into Substack's social network

This makes Beehiiv the dominant infrastructure choice for newsletter-first independent publishers in 2026. Substack still dominates at the personality-brand tier (Heather Cox Richardson at ~$1M/month, Bill Bishop's Sinocism at $1M+/year) but newsletter operators with ad-network ambitions consistently pick Beehiiv.

## Newsletter sponsorship CPMs

Local newsletter CPMs run $50–$100 per Inbox Collective, materially higher than national $20–$40. Flat-rate sponsorship sells more easily at small scale (e.g., $400/week for top sponsorship).

Newsletter sponsorship economics per the compass artifact:

| Subscriber count | Per-placement |
|---|---|
| <5K | $50–$300 flat |
| 5–20K | $250–$1,500 |
| 20–50K | $1,000–$3,000 |
| 50–100K | $3,000–$7,000 |
| 100K+ | $10,000+ standard; **Morning Brew $50,000+, TLDR ~$15K–$18K** |

Beehiiv's analytics and referral infrastructure out-of-box make it the default for hyperlocals optimizing for growth over customization.
