# Press Forward

National $500M local-news funder. Open Call grants **$100K over 2 years**.

Apply in year 1 if a hyperlocal has launch traction (100+ articles, clear revenue streams, documented human-in-the-loop review). Recommended next-cycle move in [[pier-and-point-monetization]] grant pipeline after initial revenue validation.

Press Forward prioritizes outlets serving underserved communities and uses established metrics (audience size, revenue diversification, editorial independence, AI disclosure rigor) to evaluate maturity.
