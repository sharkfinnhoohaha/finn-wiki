## TL;DR

Chicago neighborhood-coverage nonprofit news outlet. **20,000 paying subscribers (2024), 140K free newsletter subscribers, ~$3–4M combined revenue (~$1.6M philanthropic + $1M+ subscriptions).** American Journalism Project gave a $1.6M grant in January 2022. Neighborhood-resident reporters, daily morning newsletter, hyperlocal beats.

Reference comp for [[pier-and-point]]'s nonprofit-conversion path (year 2 decision in [[pier-and-point-monetization]]).

URL: <https://blockclubchicago.org>

## Why it matters for Pier and Point

- **Newsletter-first hyperlocal that converted to paid subscriptions** at scale (20K paying members). Validates the membership tier strategy.
- **Neighborhood-resident reporters** as the moat — not parachuted-in stringers. Same staffing principle Finn would apply in Ventura.
- **Nonprofit + AJP grant pipeline** is the Cityside/Berkeleyside path. Year 2 decision on whether to convert via [[institute-for-nonprofit-news|INN fiscal sponsorship]].
