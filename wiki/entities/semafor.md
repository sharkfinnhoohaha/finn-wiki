## TL;DR

Global decision-makers news brand. **$25M seed + $30M follow-on in early 2026 at $300M valuation. $40M revenue in 2025 with $2M EBITDA — first profitable year. Events drive >50% of revenue. 100+ events/year. 475 of Fortune 500 CEOs read the newsletters.**

The compass artifact's most relevant success story for [[pier-and-point]] — not because the audience matches (it doesn't) but because **events at >50% of revenue is the highest-margin lever available to a small newsroom** and Semafor proves the lever scales.

## Editorial AI

Internal **MISO tool** built on OpenAI GPT Builder by their own breaking-news reporter — *not* by Microsoft, common misreporting. Handles transcript search, cross-language discovery, "find related coverage." Editor-at-large Gina Chua's principle: anything published must be reviewed by a human editor who takes responsibility. Per-article AI assistance disclosure.
