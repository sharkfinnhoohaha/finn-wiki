# KOXR (Oxnard Airport)

Oxnard Airport — sits right on the coast, ~10mi west of [[kcam]]. Finn uses it as a cross-country destination from his home base at Camarillo. Short hop, useful for logging XC time and practicing arrivals into a Class D environment.
