# Scott Belsky

Founder of Behance, former Chief Product Officer at Adobe, prolific seed investor. Quoted by [[greg-isenberg|Isenberg]] in [[isenberg-future-of-saas-30-step]] for his framing of orchestration as the new interface layer.

## Why he's in the wiki

The Belsky tweet Isenberg reads at Step 17 became the basis for [[orchestration-as-interface]]:

> "The orchestration layer is the new interface layer as we spend our day coordinating agent workflows in a model agnostic fashion local and cloud and validating outputs human in the loop and resolving issues. The ultimate layer to own is where coordination takes place."
