## TL;DR

Document hosting + OCR + collaborative annotation platform for journalism. Standard tool for making received PDFs searchable and linkable from articles. Pairs with [[muckrock]] for the FOIA workflow.

For Pier and Point: useful once the operation receives PDF responses from CPRA requests or scrapes city agendas. OCR-then-link is more credible source-attribution than embedding screenshots.

URL: <https://www.documentcloud.org>
