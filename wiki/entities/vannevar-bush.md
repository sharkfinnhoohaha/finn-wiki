# Vannevar Bush

American engineer and science administrator. Best known for heading the U.S. Office of Scientific Research and Development during WWII and for his 1945 essay "As We May Think," published in The Atlantic Monthly.

## Relevance to this wiki

Author of the [[memex]] concept, which the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] cites as a spiritual ancestor. Bush's essay described a machine that would store a user's books and records with the ability to build associative trails between documents.

The unresolved question in Bush's essay: who builds and maintains the trails? At human scale, trail-building is tedious enough that people don't do it. The LLM Wiki pattern's claim is that LLMs now solve the maintainer problem Bush left open.

## Relevant text

"As We May Think," The Atlantic Monthly, July 1945. Widely anthologized. Still worth reading for the framing of associative versus hierarchical knowledge retrieval.
