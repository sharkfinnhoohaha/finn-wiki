# IdeaBrowser

Web tool ([ideabrowser.com](https://ideabrowser.com)) for discovering startup-idea subniches inside larger markets. Recommended by [[greg-isenberg|Isenberg]] in Step 1 of [[isenberg-future-of-saas-30-step|the 30-step playbook]] as a way to surface candidate subniches without doing all the brainstorming manually.

## How Isenberg uses it

For Step 1 — "pick a subniche inside a big market" — IdeaBrowser is positioned as one of three options alongside (a) using AI to brainstorm directly and (b) drawing from your own lived experience in a niche.

## Notes for Finn

Not yet evaluated. If pursuing the Isenberg framework on a candidate niche from Finn's surface area (aviation training admin, recording-studio booking, etc.), worth checking whether IdeaBrowser surfaces subniches Finn hasn't considered, vs. just confirming the obvious ones.
