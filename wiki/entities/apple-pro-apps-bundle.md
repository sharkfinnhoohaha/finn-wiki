# Apple Pro Apps Bundle for Education

> [!warning] Discontinued January 29, 2026
> Apple ended the one-time $199.99 education bundle and replaced it with the **Creator Studio Education subscription** ($2.99/mo or $29.99/yr). Customers who purchased before Jan 29, 2026 keep their perpetual licenses; new customers get the subscription model only.

## What it was

Until January 29, 2026, this was a $199.99 one-time education purchase that included perpetual licenses for:

- Logic Pro
- Final Cut Pro
- Motion
- Compressor
- MainStage

Lifetime licenses, no renewal, didn't expire when `.edu` lapsed. Best-in-class education deal across the entire Apple software lineup.

## What replaced it (April 2026)

**Creator Studio Education** — subscription bundle, launched January 28, 2026:

- $2.99/mo or $29.99/yr at education tier (vs. $12.99/mo or $129/yr regular)
- Same five Pro Apps + premium features for Keynote, Pages, Numbers, Freeform
- One-month free trial
- Subscription expires when `.edu` does

The economics are different — instead of $200 once for lifetime access, it's ~$30/yr while you're a student, and you lose access when you graduate. For someone who'd otherwise use Logic Pro for years, the old bundle was clearly better; the new model is a worse deal long-term.
