# Ojai Valley News + Ventura County Sun

Strongest civic accountability outfit in the Ventura region. Paywalled, but paywall-down for emergencies. Ojai-anchored with a Ventura bureau.

Publisher Laura Rearwin Ward openly calls Ventura, Oxnard, Port Hueneme **"veritable news deserts."** Runs the Ojai Valley News Foundation as a 501(c)(3). Fellow CINA member. Broke the Ventura County Parks tree-killing story (April 2024, 400+ trees, alleged 30+ herbicide violations) before [[vcstar]].

**Treat as partnership target, not competitor.** Regional cooperation (audio swaps, joint event coverage, source-sharing on countywide issues like parks/water/emergency response) makes more sense than turf war. Both outlets are supply-constrained; collaboration multiplies output.

Potential collaboration angles: [[pier-and-point]] handles daily newsletter + Ventura-only coverage, OVN/VCSun handles investigative depth.
