# Alex Krentsel

Researcher at UC Berkeley's NetSys Lab and Google Research. Creator/maintainer of [[openclaw]]. Gave the "Principles for Autonomous System Design" talk (Apr 2026) that introduced the [[loopiness-framework]] and detailed the [[openclaw-architecture|OpenClaw three-layer architecture]].

His framing of "code quality is dead, design abstractions matter more than implementation" aligns with the [[vibe-coding]] ethos already tracked in this vault.
