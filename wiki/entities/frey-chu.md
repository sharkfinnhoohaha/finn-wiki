# Frey Chu

Indie operator who builds profitable local directory websites. Self-described "encoder" (his term — a coder who works AI-first). Up-and-coming creator at the time of his February 2025 [[greg-isenberg|Isenberg]] appearance — under a few thousand YouTube subscribers, but holding multiple monetized directories built since October 2022.

## Why he's in the wiki

- Source of [[profitable-directory-pattern]] and [[static-pillar-page-directory]]. Sole methodology guide for the directory-as-mailbox-money play.
- His first directory: built October 2022, monetized for 18 consecutive months at recording, ~$2,300/mo on ~15 min/mo of upkeep. Static pillar-page format using WordPress + Elementor Pro.
- Has multiple programmatic directories beyond the static one. Mentions ranking on the first page on a fresh domain for 60K-monthly KD-70 keywords within two months on at least one programmatic build.
- Building (not yet released as of recording) an AI tool for automating directory data enrichment via Google Maps location URLs.

## Style

Tactical, no-fluff. The episode is unusual for the channel because it's all execution, no strategy meta-talk. Cites real numbers (his first directory's revenue, his big data sheet's 119K → 5K row prune, his "$5–7K/mo on 85K traffic" estimates for cited examples).

Calls Claude "Cloe" on-air. Greg ribs him about it.
