## TL;DR

Free API for state-level legislator and bill data across all 50 US states. Used by civic news operators for state-level coverage. Pairs with [[civic-scraper]] (which covers municipal/county) to give end-to-end government-data coverage at the state and below.

For Pier and Point: relevant for **California Assembly + State Senate coverage that touches Ventura County** (district 36, district 19, etc.) — not the daily core, but useful for tracking state-level decisions affecting the area.

URL: <https://openstates.org>
