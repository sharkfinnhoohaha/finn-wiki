# Lindy AI

Proactive executive-assistant agent that lives in iMessage and Slack. Triages email, manages calendars, and acts without explicit prompts (Phase 3 by [[phase-3-agents|the briefing's taxonomy]]).

## Why it's worth tracking

Closest commercial analog to the kind of personal-assistant layer Finn could build on top of his [[notion-life-os]]. Two patterns to watch:

1. **iMessage as agent surface.** Source *"How I use iMessage and AI to run my life"* (in both [[notebooklm-overlook-strategy]] and [[notebooklm-simple-ui-viral-ai]]) argues iMessage is the right substrate for personal AI because it's where everyone already operates. Lindy operationalizes that.
2. **Proactivity, not reactivity.** Lindy's hook is acting before being asked — flagging emails that need replies, surfacing calendar conflicts, drafting follow-ups. Same posture as the autonomy section of [[wat-framework-marketing]].

## How it might fit Finn's stack

Not a replacement for Cowork or Claude Code; a different layer. If Finn ever productizes personal-assistant as a service ([[ai-agency-niches]]), Lindy is the competitor. If he ever wires up an iMessage MCP, Lindy is the reference for what "proactive" looks like in that channel.
