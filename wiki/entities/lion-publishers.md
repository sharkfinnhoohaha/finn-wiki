# LION Publishers

Local Independent Online News trade organization. Membership $140/year micro tier.

Unlocks: media liability insurance group discount, Sustainability Audit eligibility ($20K grant), free legal hotline access for member newsrooms.

**Key data from 2024 survey:** Median annual revenue for members over 1 year old: **$147,843, +13.7% YoY**. Newsrooms with dedicated revenue staff earn 7× the median of those without. Empirical inflection point to "sustainable" operation is **3+ established revenue streams** (memberships, sponsorships, grants, events, etc.).

Critical first-week join for any new hyperlocal. LION is the entry point to the grant ecosystem (Press Forward, GNI, Knight Foundation all favor LION members) and provides the baseline insurance discount that makes liability manageable at solo-founder scale.
