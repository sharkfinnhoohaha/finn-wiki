## TL;DR

Product management newsletter + podcast + community. **$4M+/year minimum** — $2M+ from $15/mo paid newsletter (18,000 paid subs at ~5% conversion of 1.2M total) plus podcast advertising. **Community of 10,000 members across 193 meetups in 30 countries.**

Reference for **paid-newsletter conversion math** (5% conversion of free → paid is the realistic ceiling Pier and Point's [[pier-and-point-monetization|membership tier]] should plan against) and for **community-as-moat** as a sustaining-the-paid-tier mechanism.
