# John DePaola Quartet

Jazz quartet, performers on **"Touch of Blue"** (composer [[brad-hatfield]]). Trumpet-led. Used as a track candidate on Finn's Berklee Music Supervision assignment for [[alchemy]]. Finn noted the album has two versions of "Touch of Blue" — a guitar version and a trumpet version — and asked which one was being referenced.
