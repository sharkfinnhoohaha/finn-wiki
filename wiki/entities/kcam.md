# KCAM (Camarillo Airport)

Camarillo Airport, the home base for [[finn-bennett]]'s flight training. Located ~15mi east of Ventura. All of Finn's pattern work, instruction, and most training flights launch from here. Tracked in the [[notion-life-os]] under the Aviation area, with the category `Camarillo Instruction` for instructor-fee line items.

## Activity captured

- Pattern work
- Cross-country to [[koxr]]
- Night currency
- Instrument approaches under foggles
