## TL;DR

FOIA workflow management platform. Manages requests, tracks responses, hosts received documents. Standard tool for newsrooms that file public records requests at any cadence.

For Pier and Point: relevant once original-reporting cadence is established (months 6+) and the operation starts filing CPRA requests against Ventura County agencies. Pairs with [[documentcloud]] for OCR + annotation of received PDFs.

URL: <https://www.muckrock.com>
