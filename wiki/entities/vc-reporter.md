# VC Reporter

Times Media Group weekly, free, ~35,000 print copies distributed in Ventura.

Decent arts and lifestyle coverage, limited civic accountability. No daily digital presence, no investigative desk.

**Partnership target for [[pier-and-point]] on arts/music cross-promotion.** VC Reporter's strength in restaurant reviews, gallery openings, theater coverage complements [[pier-and-point]]'s focus on civic/breaking news. Potential syndication swap: VC Reporter runs a excerpt of [[pier-and-point]] investigative stories to their print audience, [[pier-and-point]] cross-links VC Reporter arts roundups.

Cooperative play increases both outlets' reach; zero-sum competition diminishes both.
