# Source — student discount research, April 2026

Compiled April 28, 2026 from web research + the live GitHub Student Developer Pack page (https://education.github.com/pack). Driving question: *what's worth claiming through the [[berklee-online|Berklee Online]] `.edu` while it's valid?*

## TL;DR

- GitHub Student Pack has ~86 active offers as of April 2026. ~12 perpetual, ~22 lifetime-while-student, ~35 one-year, ~20 short trial, ~8 one-time.
- Highest stack-fit for Finn's [[next-js-patterns|Next.js]] / [[sanity-patterns|Sanity]] / [[clerk-auth-pattern|Clerk]] / [[railway-deployment|Railway]] reality: Copilot, JetBrains, Clerk Pro, DigitalOcean credit, Sentry, Datadog, Stripe waiver.
- Outside the Pack, the highest-leverage one-time deals are **Apple Pro Apps Bundle** ($200 lifetime for Logic + Final Cut), **Native Instruments Komplete** ($99 one-time), **Ableton Live Suite** ($99 one-time), and **Spotify Premium Student** ($5.99 locked for 4 years from activation).
- Hard deadline: **GitHub Certification voucher expires June 30, 2026**.

## Method

- Pulled the live offer index from `education.github.com/pack`, parsed all 86 offers.
- Web-searched April 2026 status of major non-Pack programs (Apple, JetBrains direct, Spotify, Adobe CC, Notion, Figma, music software).
- Cross-referenced against Finn's stack from [[hardware-setup]], [[next-js-patterns]], [[clerk-auth-pattern]], [[overlook-strategy-positioning]], [[overlook-audio-positioning]].
- Items flagged `[VERIFY]` on the main page are programs where pricing fluctuates (Cursor, Anthropic student tiers, Splice) — don't assume the rate is current.
