## TL;DR

Three niche-targeted landing pages generated by a scheduled task overnight, intended to deploy to [[finn-v2-portfolio]] (Next.js 14 + Sanity) and rank for `[city] + [niche]` long-tail keywords. The batch is a concrete tactical answer to the open positioning question on [[overlook-strategy-positioning]] — it picks vertical authority over generic agency framing. Raw files at `raw/landing-pages/2026-04-26-overlook-vertical-landing-pages/`. Canonical Sanity-ready copies at `outputs/landing-pages/`.

## Cluster rationale (from web search)

- **Yacht broker** — page 1 owned by national template SaaS (Relevance Yacht, Charternet, VEVS). Zero Ventura/Channel Islands local agency in the SERP despite 10+ active brokers in the harbor. Direct case-study fit. Lowest competition.
- **Boutique vacation rental** — Whitestone, Bethany Works, Local Creative dominate the broad term, but the boutique + small-portfolio + direct-booking angle has thinner results. SERP leans SaaS (Lodgify, Goliday). Sømliøya is a defensible case.
- **Winery / tasting room** — generic "wine industry web design" PPC plus winery brand sites themselves. No specialist owns it for SB / SLO / Ventura county. No closed case yet, framed as "third vertical opening 2026" with founder pricing for first two clients.

Considered but rejected: charter-boat / sportfishing (more crowded SERP — Boat Marketing Pros, Lounge Lizard) and surf-shop ecommerce (Surf City Web Design owns OC tier).

## Format / deployment recommendation

**Sanity ingestion, not `page.tsx`.** These are pure structured content (hero, value-prop sections, process steps, pricing tiers, FAQ) that map cleanly to Sanity schemas, and the copy will iterate more often than the layout. Coupling to a deploy via `page.tsx` is the wrong tradeoff. Same logic that put [[three-altitudes]] on Sanity already.

## Strategic implications

This batch operationalizes a few existing wiki ideas:
- The **vertical productization** thread on [[overlook-strategy-positioning]] (open positioning question, April 2026). Picks vertical authority as the front-door.
- The **media-first distribution** idea ([[Finn-OS/ideas/backlog]] entry, 2026-04-25) — landing pages as the SEO scaffolding for a daily content engine in those verticals.
- The **niche subniche selection** discipline from [[isenberg-future-of-saas-30-step]] — three picks based on real SERP gaps, not vibes.

It does NOT operationalize:
- Per-task pricing ([[per-task-pricing]]) — these pages are still project-priced, not per-task. A future iteration could add a per-task line below the Foundation tier (e.g. "single landing page — $1,500").
- Agent-fulfilled SaaS ([[agent-native-saas]]) — these are services, not productized SaaS.

## Open threads

- **Sanity schemas** for `landingPage` document type don't exist on [[finn-v2-portfolio]] yet. Estimated 1–2 hour build before ingestion is possible.
- **Booking link** on the CTAs (`#book`) needs a real Calendly / Cal.com URL before deploy.
- **Process timing claims** ("ranks for X inside 90 days") are aspirational — should add a footnote disclaimer or back off the language before publishing.
- **Winery founder-pricing offer** is good through Q3 2026 per the page copy. If Finn doesn't close two winery clients by Sept 30, revisit the framing.
