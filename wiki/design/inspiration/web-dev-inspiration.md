## Read against the design system

The references skew editorial, minimal, high-motion, aviation-adjacent. [beyond-aero.com](https://www.beyond-aero.com/) and [jal.com](https://www.jal.com/) line up with the [[design-system/overview|Overlook Strategy aesthetic]]: restrained, cool-shifted, generous whitespace, mono meta. The animation references ([daxarts.com](https://www.daxarts.com/), [landonorris.com](https://landonorris.com/), [Igloo Inc.](https://www.igloo.inc/)) push more motion than the Overlook system allows: useful as reach goals, but the system caps motion at 240ms fades and 16px lifts.

[scoutmotors.com](https://www.scoutmotors.com/) under typography & color points to a similar editorial-with-utility instinct that drives the serif + display-mono pairing in the system.
