# Weekly review

Rolling log. **Newest at the top.** One H2 per week, dated by Friday or Sunday.

## Format

```
## Week of YYYY-MM-DD

### Shipped
- 

### Stalled / blocked
- 

### Captured (new ideas, conversations)
- 

### Energy / mood
- (one or two lines of honest read on how the week felt)

### Next week's 1-3 priorities
- 
```

## Why this exists

A weekly review is the smallest unit where patterns become visible. One week is too small to see drift; one month is too big to remember details. Friday or Sunday, 15 minutes, top of the page.

If you skip 2+ weeks in a row, don't try to backfill — just start the next one and note the gap.
