# Monthly review

Rolling log. **Newest at the top.** One H2 per month, written first week of the next month.

## Format

```
## YYYY-MM

### Themes
- (2-3 sentences on what the month was actually about)

### Wins
- 

### Misses / regrets
- 

### Decisions made
- 

### Money in / money out (high level)
- 

### Goals progress
- Pull from [[../career/goals]] — which 6mo / 2yr items moved?

### Next month's intent
- 
```

## Why this exists

A month is the right unit for goal-tracking and pattern-spotting. The weekly review is tactical; the monthly review is strategic.
