# Goals

Where you're trying to go. Update monthly. Be specific about timeframes — vague goals stop being useful fast.

## 6-month horizon (now → ~Oct 2026)

> _What you want true by end of October. Concrete enough to know if it happened._

- 

## 2-year horizon (~April 2028)

> _Where Overlook Strategy + Overlook Audio + you personally are. Revenue, role, lifestyle, location._

- 

## 5-year horizon (~April 2031)

> _The big-picture version. Brand, scale, geography, who you're working with, what you're building._

- 

## What you've already decided

> _Decisions you've made that constrain choices. Things like "no startup co-founder situations", "no relocating before X", "Overlook is the brand even if I do other things". Lock these in so you stop re-litigating._

- 

## Tensions to resolve

> _Things in conflict. Tradeoffs you haven't made yet. Don't pretend they're settled._

-
